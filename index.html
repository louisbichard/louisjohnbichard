Final year project


Background


My final year project is for an unnamed government funded charity. The system is a that sits centrally between many other external services: Charities, law enforcement, councillors and acts as a management tool for these services

As users are referred to third parties, these referalls can be managed and rated so that data is fed back to government for performance and success metrics.

The system initially is a beta prototype used to scope out areas of technical difficulty, and to experiment with a number of different designs and concepts

The system is to be piloted in one area of the UK, but will be scaled across the country if successfully, and is architected to reflect this requirement


System Overview


The system is a dashboard like application consisting of 2 distinct parts which will share architecture, design look and feel however each will expose differing functionality respectively dependent on the type of user: internal or external, and the users role
